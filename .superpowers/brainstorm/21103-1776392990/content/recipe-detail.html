Butter Chicken
A rich, aromatic dish of tender chicken simmered in a velvety tomato-cream sauce. One of India's most beloved exports — deeply spiced, perfectly balanced.
Prep time
45 min
Difficulty
Medium
Servings
4
Cuisine
Indian

600g ✓ have

3 tbsp ✓ have

200ml ✓ have
Canned tomatoes 400g
Garlic
4 cloves ✓ have
Garam masala
2 tsp ✓ have
Instructions
1
Cut chicken breast into 4cm chunks. Season with half the garam masala, salt, and a squeeze of lemon. Set aside to marinate for at least 15 minutes.
Longer marinating (up to 2 hours in the fridge) makes a noticeable difference to flavour depth.
2
Melt butter in a heavy-bottomed pan over medium-high heat. Sear the chicken pieces until golden on all sides, about 3 minutes per side. Remove and set aside.
3
In the same pan, add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant. Add canned tomatoes and remaining garam masala. Simmer for 10 minutes until thickened.
4
Return chicken to the pan, pour in cream, and stir gently. Simmer on low for 10 minutes until the sauce coats the back of a spoon. Season to taste.
Don't let it boil after adding cream — keep it at a gentle simmer.
